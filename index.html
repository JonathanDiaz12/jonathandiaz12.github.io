Who are the Circuit Sorcerers?

We are an enthusiastic community of students and alumni coming together to create a new FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team, eager to explore the realm of STEM in the form of hands-on learning. Our mission is to provide all members with the chance to foster hands-on, real-world engineering, programming, design, and teamwork skills, preparing us not only for competition, but for our futures.

What sets us apart is our creative drive: we aim to inject a powerful visual presence into all we do, from our robot to our presence at competitions. Now, because we are a rookie team starting in the 2026 robotics season, we don't have a lot of experience to show that we have achieved those goals mention previously. However that doesn't mean that we aren't going to try and give it our all!
